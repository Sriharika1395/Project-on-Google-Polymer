ABOUT THE CLUB:

The CBCC was formulated on 22nd september 2007. It works towards bringing out the cultural talents and provides a platform for the students to showcase their talents. It is a regular platform for staging various social and cultural events thereby inducing national integrity. The main event of CBCC is the annual day celebrations of the college, Shruthi.
