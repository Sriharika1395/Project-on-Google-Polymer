ABOUT THE CLUB:

The Google Student Club at CBIT is a platform that provides opportunities to students to improve their skills in technical and marketing areas.Students who are a part of the Google Student Club will get to attend many different workshops and learn things related to Android App Development, Cloud Computing, Digital Marketing and many more directly from Googlers! The Google Student Club also aims at empowering the students with tools that will help them do amazing things to help the society around them.
