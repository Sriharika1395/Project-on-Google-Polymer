ABOUT THE CLUB:

Chaitanya Smruti, CBIT ALumni association was started in 1983 after the first batch graduated out to enter the unknown world. Since then the alumni have kept up their friendships and bonds with fellowmates. The alumni associaton has an active alumni board that is involved in aligning the mission of the organization and pursuing the short and long term goals.
